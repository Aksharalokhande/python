About Government Polytechnic Hingoli

Government Polytechnic Hingoli was established in 2009 under the Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra. The institute is approved by AICTE and affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE). The campus is located at MIDC Limbala, Hingoli and provides quality technical education to students across the region.

The institute offers diploma programs in engineering disciplines such as -

  • Computer Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering.

Government Polytechnic Hingoli focuses on academic excellence, skill development and industry-oriented technical education to prepare students for successful careers.

With modern infrastructure, laboratories, library facilities and a student-focused learning environment, the institute aims to transform human lives through quality technical education and sustainable development.

Project Objective

The objective of this project is to provide a centralized platform where students and faculty can access academic information, notices, schedules, and results in an organized manner.
